Ranking System

Our rankings reflect both current performance and future potential of Gulf basketball players, including immigrant players.

Ranking Factors

  • Performance (30%) – Game statistics, tournament performance, and consistency.
  • Skill Level (25%) – Shooting, passing, defense, basketball IQ, and versatility.
  • Physical Tools (20%) – Height, wingspan, agility, and athleticism.
  • Competition Level (15%) – Strength of leagues and tournaments played.
  • Projection / Potential (10%) – Likelihood to improve and reach higher levels.

Update Schedule

Rankings are updated regularly after major tournaments and league seasons. Each update will display the date for transparency.
